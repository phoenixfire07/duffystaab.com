Duffy & Staab LLC is a law practice that capitalizes on our attorneys’ experience at large law firms, major corporations and in the public sector to provide you quality legal service at a competitive cost.

With the addition of Judge Kevin Thomas Duffy as of counsel in 2017, Duffy & Staab LLC is one of the only small law firms in the area with a retired federal court judge on its staff.

Our goal is to combine that experience with innovative thinking and a common sense, practical approach to solving your legal problems.

When you need professionalism and attorneys who get the job done, but want someone who speaks your language, it’s time for Duffy & Staab LLC.

With attorneys admitted to practice in New York, Connecticut and New Jersey, we are certain to be able to meet your needs throughout the tri-state area.
